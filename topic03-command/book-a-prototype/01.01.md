|  1 |         a |        a |     a |        a |

pm> cu b b b b
+----+-----------+----------+-------+----------+
| ID | FIRSTNAME | LASTNAME | EMAIL | PASSWORD |

|  2 |         b |        b |     b |        b |

pm> undo
